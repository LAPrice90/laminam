APPLICATIONS

Large-format slabs create seamless interior wall cladding with near-invisible joints and easy maintenance.

Ultra-dense surfaces deliver hard-wearing, low-porosity floors for high-traffic commercial areas.

Heat-, scratch- and stain-resistant porcelain tops give sleek, hygienic work surfaces for hospitality and residential kitchens.

Bespoke furniture — tables, shelving and reception desks — fabricated for durable, luxury finishes.

Lightweight, UV-stable panels clad façades with modern stone aesthetics while meeting performance specs.

Monolithic vanity tops and sculptural basins in matching porcelain, impervious to cosmetics and limescale.

Single-piece, slip-resistant trays create flush wet-rooms; colour-matched to wall panels for a “total look”.

Thin yet rigid treads + risers give continuous veining and high abrasion resistance on commercial stairs.

Scratch-proof 3–5 mm skins bond to doors and drawer fronts, keeping cabinetry light yet ultra-durable.

A-rated non-combustible panels withstand >300 °C, perfect for modern fireplace surrounds and hearths.

Large-format porcelain or stone tops on steel or timber bases—ideal for restaurants, boardrooms, and luxury retail displays.
