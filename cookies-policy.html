Last updated: 1 July 2024

Sabre Stone Commercial uses cookies on this website (sabrestonecommercial.com) to improve your browsing experience and to understand how our site is used.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences (such as login, language, and other settings).

How we use cookies:

  • To remember if you’ve accepted our cookie notice
  • To gather anonymous usage statistics through Google Analytics
  • To ensure the website functions correctly

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the site to work (e.g., remembering your cookie preferences).
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site so we can improve your experience. (You can refuse analytics cookies through the cookie banner.)

Managing cookies:

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser’s cookies or adjusting your browser settings. Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies, but this may affect how the website works.
