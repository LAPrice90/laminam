Directions

From Cranleigh: Take the B2128 signposted to Horsham by the Esso Garage. Continue for about 1.9 miles, and the site is on the right.

From Horsham: Take the Rudgwick turning and drive for about 2.5 miles on B2128. The site is on the left.

When approaching, look for “Farm Entrance” to Baynards Farm. Continue for a quarter of a mile along the track on the right-hand side. It is the last farm building on the right with parking outside.
