About Us

Supplying Large-Format Porcelain for the Built Environment

Laminam Commercial UK is the authorised UK distributor for Laminam S.p.A, Italy’s leader in large-format porcelain surfaces. We work with architects, designers and developers to supply high-performance materials for walls, furniture, worktops and façades that combine beauty and durability.

Our focus is on specification and support. We help with product selection, sampling and technical advice to make sure every surface performs as expected. All recommendations are based on Laminam’s proven materials and detailed product data.

Where quality meets practicality

Laminam porcelain is designed to look elegant and perform reliably. Each surface is resistant to scratches, stains, heat and UV light. It stays beautiful even in busy spaces such as hotels, restaurants and residential developments.

As the UK partner for Laminam, we assist with sampling, installation guidance and advice on finishes, thicknesses and formats. Whether you are creating a feature wall or completing a large commercial project, our goal is clear communication and dependable results.

Rooted in tradition, focused on innovation

From concept to completion across the UK

Our team combines material knowledge with practical understanding of project delivery. We work closely with specifiers at each stage, offering advice on formats, fixing systems and performance criteria.

Through our partnership with Laminam in Italy, we bring cutting-edge surface technology to the UK market. The result is a collection of materials that balance design quality, environmental responsibility and long-term performance.

Every collaboration aims for efficiency and precision, helping clients achieve durable, elegant finishes that stand the test of time.

Designed to perform, built to impress

Every detail, every interface, every finish.

We act as a collaborative partner, from early samples and mock-ups to final handover. Our team provides guidance on installation methods, joint details and maintenance to ensure lasting performance.

Laminam products are made under strict quality controls and supported by comprehensive technical documentation. We help you choose suitable finishes and thicknesses, aligning specification with practical delivery.

Sustainability is part of everything we do. Laminam’s production processes minimise waste and extend product life, supporting both environmental goals and long-term value for our clients.
